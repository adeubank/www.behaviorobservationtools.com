Frequently Asked Questions

How do I export my observation data?

You can export observation data directly from the app as PDF reports or CSV files for further analysis. Look for the export button in your observation details.

Is student data secure?

Yes, all student data remains on your device by default. Data is only transmitted for crash reporting or technical support when explicitly needed, and is never retained for more than 14 days.

Can I customize observation intervals?

Absolutely! BOSS allows you to customize both observation intervals and total duration to match your specific assessment needs and methodological requirements.

Do you offer training resources?

We provide comprehensive documentation and best practices guidance. For specific training needs, please contact us and we'll be happy to help or recommend resources.

Is BOSS compatible with my device?

BOSS is available for both iOS and Android devices. Check the app store requirements for specific version compatibility with your device.

Can I use BOSS for virtual observations?

Yes! BOSS includes specialized features for virtual observations, including tracking student participation in remote learning environments.
